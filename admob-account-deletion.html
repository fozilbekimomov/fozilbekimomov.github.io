Most of your data lives only on your device. Deleting the app or signing out instantly removes local cache, OAuth tokens, and authentication state. Anonymized analytics on Google's side can be removed by request — see Method 3 below.

Quick Reference

You have three independent ways to delete your account and associated data. Method 1 is the fastest and works fully offline.

1 Delete from inside the app (recommended)
  1. Open AdMob Revenue Tracker on your device.
  2. Tap the avatar in the top-right → Settings.
  3. Scroll down to Account.
  4. Tap Sign Out & Delete Local Data.
  5. Confirm in the dialog — done.
Effect: Hive cache (revenue, eCPM, impressions), OAuth access & refresh tokens, settings, and notification preferences are wiped from your device immediately.
2 Revoke access from your Google Account
  1. Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  2. Find AdMob Revenue Tracker in the list of third-party apps.
  3. Click Remove Access.
  4. Confirm — Google revokes our OAuth refresh token within minutes.

This stops AdMob Revenue Tracker from accessing your AdMob data. You should also use Method 1 to clear the local cache.

3 Email deletion request (server-side analytics)

If you also want anonymized analytics records (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics) deleted, send an email request:

To: fozilbekimomov@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: Your Google account email used to sign in.

We respond within 14 days and confirm deletion. By default, Firebase analytics auto-purge after 2 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will deleting the app from my phone also delete my data?

Yes. Uninstalling AdMob Revenue Tracker removes all local data (cache, tokens, settings) from your device. However, the OAuth permission on Google's side persists — use Method 2 to revoke it.

Do I need a paid account to delete?

No. The app is free, and account deletion is also free and unconditional.

Can I delete only part of my data without deleting my account?

The app does not maintain a server-side account, so this question maps to: Firebase Analytics retention is set to 2 months (auto-purge). To request earlier deletion of analytics events while keeping local cache, email fozilbekimomov@gmail.com.

How long until my data is fully gone?

  • On-device data: instant (Method 1 or app uninstall).
  • OAuth permission: within minutes (Method 2).
  • Firebase analytics: within 14 days of email request, otherwise auto-purge in 2 months.

Частые вопросы

Если я удалю приложение с телефона, удалятся ли данные?

Да. Удаление AdMob Revenue Tracker стирает все локальные данные (кэш, токены, настройки) с устройства. Но OAuth-разрешение на стороне Google остаётся — отзовите его Способом 2.

Нужен ли платный аккаунт для удаления?

Нет. Приложение бесплатное, и удаление аккаунта тоже бесплатное и безусловное.

Можно ли удалить только часть данных, не удаляя аккаунт?

В приложении нет серверного аккаунта, поэтому вопрос сводится к: ретенция Firebase Analytics установлена на 2 месяца (авто-удаление). Чтобы запросить более раннее удаление событий аналитики при сохранении локального кэша, напишите на fozilbekimomov@gmail.com.

За какое время данные полностью исчезают?

  • Локальные данные: мгновенно (Способ 1 или удаление приложения).
  • OAuth-разрешение: в течение нескольких минут (Способ 2).
  • Firebase Analytics: в течение 14 дней по email-запросу, иначе авто-удаление за 2 месяца.
