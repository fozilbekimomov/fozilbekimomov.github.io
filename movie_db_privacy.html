Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Movie DB ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or directly provide personal information. However, we automatically collect certain data through integrated third-party services.

1.1 Device and Technical Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Language and region settings

1.2 Usage Data

  • App interactions (screens visited, features used)
  • Session duration
  • App performance metrics

1.3 Diagnostics Data

  • Crash logs
  • Error reports

2. How We Collect Information

Data is collected automatically via third-party SDKs integrated into the app.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID

  • IP address
  • Interaction with ads

Learn more: Google Ads Policy

3.2 Firebase Analytics

Used to understand user behavior and improve the app.

3.3 Firebase Crashlytics

Used to monitor app stability and fix crashes.

4. How We Use Information

  • To display personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • To detect and fix technical issues

5. Data Sharing

We may share collected information with third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase.

We do NOT sell your personal data.

6. Legal Basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (analytics, app improvement)
  • User consent (advertising personalization where applicable)

7. Data Retention

We retain collected data only as long as necessary for analytics and app improvement purposes.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS).

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
